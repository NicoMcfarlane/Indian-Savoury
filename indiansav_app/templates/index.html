About Us

Indian Savoury is a tiffin food service in the Niagara region that provides delicious and authentic Indian Cuisine dishes. We offer a wide variety of traditional meals, sides, and drinks at affordable prices. As well, try our tasty signature vegetarian tiffin or customize your dish with the build your own tiffin option.

History

Indian Savoury opened in 1990 by a team of five chefs. They started with the goal of providing spicy Tiffin meals for all. Today we act as a food delivery and pick-up service to serve authentic Indian cuisine to the area. We bring a little taste of India to the Niagara Region.

The Food

The tiffin meals provide simple and comforting meals for all to enjoy that is full of flavour. Ranging from large meals to small, personal meals at an affordable price while reducing the environmental impact. Indian Savoury is a unique way to get great quality take out that stays hot and can be enjoyed anywhere.
